SUB GOAL 1: Fix my social awkwardness

Approach is maybe to keep a journal with daily tasks as well as a "Parking lot" section to keep tracks of the people I know. I think that maybe this should be a physical notebook.
Some tasks to work on this:

  • At the end of the day, write about 3 interactions that happened and answer the following questions. 1) What did I feel? 2) What caused the feeling? 3) How did I respond?
  • If an especially difficult interaction happened. Journal about it and try and analyze how I sounded.
  • Add three check boxes for open-ended questions. These could be about Family, Occupation, Recreation, Dreams (F-O-R-D Method), be a situation tie in, or a follow up.

Ideally the last point will be getting recorded in the "Parking Lot".
Here are some tips for being better at remembering people and networking.

  • Use 3x Rule for names. Say name as soon as you hear it.
  • Say it again in the conversation
  • Say it after leaving the coversation and add one key detail. Record this in the Parking Lot
  • In parking lot record: Name & Date met
  • Where/Context
  • 1 Personal Detail
  • 1 Potential Follow-Up Topic
  • Add a reminder in the reminder queue in my planner, for Core Coworkers follow up weekly, for General Coworkers follow up every 4-8 weeks, and for my extended network follow up every 3-6 months.
  • With a new entry I should follow up within 1-2 weeks
  • When the follow-up window opens start researching a way to have a value-driven check in. This could be shared value (industry article, book, or video). A congratulation on something. Or asking for advice or feedback.
  • To open an interaction use a common ground such as the immediate environment, asking for help, or give a sincere compliment.
